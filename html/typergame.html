Typing Wizard

Description:

Built from scratch in Java using JFrame, Typing Wizard is a fully functional typing game, complete with profiles, gamemodes, stats, and theme options.

Choose between a set number of words or timed mode, and type as fast as you can to get a high WPS.

Insipired by my love for TyperRacer and MonkeyType

A cursor follows where you are, and missed words will be underlined.

See your stats, and all previous games.

Change the gamemode, play with previously missed words, and change the word-count or time.

Load and save profiles.

You can even change the colors and font.
